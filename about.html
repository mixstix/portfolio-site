Creating great stuff simply for the love of it

I've been a maker ever since I could remember. As a kid, I would be dreaming, making, imagining, playing with anything I could get my hands on - playdoh, paper, mattresses and bedsheets (they make really great forts!), paint, beads -- anything really. As I got older all that creative energy was channeled into another thing that I loved to play around with: computers.

I started graphic designing about 6 years ago, and have been creating ever since. The software I use are Photoshop, Illustrator for design, Wordpress if I need a content management system, HTML/CSS and jQuery for coding up websites. I’m able to make designs responsive and also know Git version control.

My design style always consists of artistic elements, and I always try to incorporate something that is distinctly Singaporean. My other portfolio features a photograph from Haji Lane in Singapore. This current portfolio site features a heartland photo with HDB flats in the background and a public car park in the foreground. (Did you see the cool sunrise effect? If not, go back to look at it again!) I’m not really sure about the location of this photo but someone did identify it as somewhere in Serangoon. I’ll be sure to find out!

Other than design, I love listening to or making music, looking out for new inspiration, talking to people in the same industry, reading and writing, and unwinding with a slow drive. Driving with music on is something that really relaxes me.

If you have a project that I could help you out with, or just want to chat about anything, shoot me an email!
